标题

SeaWorld: theme park results show queues forming for in-person entertainment

海洋世界：主题乐园展现了一个为了线下娱乐而排队的场景

  • in-person entertainment：表示需要真人到场的线下娱乐

副标题

Healthy revenues prove that the outdoor centre is recovering well from Covid restrictions

一个健康的财务使得户外中心在新冠的影响下恢复了过来

全文

Americans are clamouring to walk through turnstiles, jump on to roller coasters and gawk at dolphins. They are even happy to pay more for the privilege. On Thursday, SeaWorld Entertainment reported 2021 fourth-quarter results that trumped the same quarter in pre-pandemic 2019.
Park attendance at the company’s dozen facilities climbed 5 per cent to 5mn people. Those visitors were more than willing to empty their wallets once inside the gates. Total revenue per capita jumped 18 per cent, driven by food and merchandise purchases.
Theme park attendance has been surprisingly strong at media conglomerates too. Walt Disney and Comcast have long touted their digital streaming services. This is where most investor interest lies. Yet the recent rebound at Disney World and Comcast’s Universal Studios created big profits to plough back into their respective money-sucking video units.
The best may be still to come. Foreign tourists have yet to return in force to the US. At the same time, companies believe that efficiencies wrung out during the pandemic have lowered structural operating costs. At SeaWorld alone, operating profit nearly doubled relative to the last quarter of 2019.
Theme parks may be uniquely positioned to exploit the recovery from Covid-19. They tend to be outdoors or open-air and can hold tens of thousands of visitors a day.
As for operating costs, all service businesses are facing labour shortages and higher wages. But Disney noted that its theme park business may have found some permanent efficiencies. Hotel check-ins have now been automated and newly created mobile dining, ordering by app, requires fewer staff.
Expect theme park attendance to remain strong throughout 2022 as Americans make up for lost time and international travellers return for holidays. SeaWorld shares are up more than seven times since their bottom in March 2020, making it one of the top reopening bets. Unfortunately, investors arriving at the attraction at this late stage might be stuck at the back of the line.

第一段

Americans are clamouring to walk through turnstiles*, jump on to roller coasters and gawk at dolphins. They are even happy to pay more for the privilege. On Thursday, SeaWorld Entertainment reported 2021 fourth-quarter results that trumped the same quarter in pre-pandemic 2019.

  • clamour：n. 吵闹；喧闹；v. 大声的要求
  • turnstile：旋转门
  • roller coaster：过山车
  • gawk：n. 笨人；v. 呆呆的看着；物理的瞪眼看
  • dolphin：海豚

美国人大声要求着经过了旋转门，跳上过山车并且呆呆的看着海豚。他们甚至为了特权多付钱而开心。在礼拜四，海洋世界公布了2021年的第四个季度的财务报告，其中宣布和疫情前的2019年达到相同水平。

第二段

Park attendance at the company’s dozen facilities climbed 5 per cent to 5mn people. Those visitors were more than willing to empty their wallets once inside the gates. Total revenue per capita jumped 18 per cent, driven by food and merchandise purchases.

海洋世界在公司的十二个场所里对于五百万的人上涨了五个百分点。一旦这些游客进入了大门就会更愿意的清空他们的钱包。平均总财报上涨了十八个百分点，都是由于食物和商品的购买。

第三段

Theme park attendance has been surprisingly strong at media conglomerates too. Walt Disney and Comcast have long touted their digital streaming services. This is where most investor interest lies. Yet the recent rebound at Disney World and Comcast’s Universal Studios created big profits to plough back into their respective money-sucking video units.

  • conglomerate：砾岩；企业集团；联合大公司
  • tout：兜售；推销；吹捧
  • rebound：回弹
  • plough back into：重新投入
    • plough into：投入

主题乐园的加入在媒体大公司中也是令人惊讶的。Walt Disney和Comcast已经长时间的推销他们的数字直播。这是大多数的投资者所感兴趣的。但是在Disney World和Comcast's Universal Studios的回弹创造了一个大的利润去重新投入资金到金钱吸尘器般的影视单元。

第四段

The best may be still to come. Foreign tourists have yet to return in force to the US. At the same time, companies believe that efficiencies wrung out during the pandemic have lowered structural operating costs. At SeaWorld alone, operating profit nearly doubled relative to the last quarter of 2019.

  • wrung：wring的过去式，心中有烦恼的
    • wring：n. 拧；紧紧握手；v. 拧

最好的终会来的。国外的旅行者们任然不得不强制的回到美国。同时公司相信，在疫情期间的高效的压榨将会产生耕地的结构操作成本。对于SeaWorld，操作利润是2019年最后一个季度的两倍。

第五段

Theme parks may be uniquely positioned to exploit the recovery from Covid-19. They tend to be outdoors or open-air and can hold tens of thousands of visitors a day.

主题公园可能是唯一在新冠疫情恢复期间开放的地点。他们更倾向于户外，并且他们可以承受一天之内数万人的来访。

第六段

As for operating costs, all service businesses are facing labour shortages and higher wages. But Disney noted that its theme park business may have found some permanent efficiencies. Hotel check-ins have now been automated and newly created mobile dining, ordering by app, requires fewer staff.

  • as for：关于；对于

对于操作成本，所有的服务行业都在面临劳动力短缺和高的工资。但是迪斯尼公司指出他们的主题公园可能已经发现了一些永久的高效发方法。旅馆的签到服务现在已经有自动的新的移动餐饮，通过app点餐，只需要更少的员工。

第七段

Expect theme park attendance to remain strong throughout 2022 as Americans make up for lost time and international travellers return for holidays. SeaWorld shares are up more than seven times since their bottom in March 2020, making it one of the top reopening bets. Unfortunately, investors arriving at the attraction at this late stage might be stuck at the back of the line.

  • make up：弥补；起草

当美国人弥补失去的时间和节假日的旅行是令人期待的主题公园依旧保持强劲在2022年。海上世界的股份是2020年五月最低股份的五倍，使其成为重新开启的打赌。不幸的是，在这么晚的阶段的吸引力的投资者可能会被困在队伍的最后端。
