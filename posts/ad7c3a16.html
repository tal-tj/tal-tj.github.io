Ultrathin Cellulose Voronoi-Nanonet Membranes Enable High-Flux and Energy-Saving Water Purification翻译

发表于 2022-02-24 分类于 English
本文字数： 9.8k 阅读时长 ≈ 9 分钟

标题

Ultrathin Cellulose Voronoi-Nanonet Membranes Enable High-Flux and Energy-Saving Water Purification

具有高通量和低能耗的超薄纤维素Voronoi纳米网薄膜水净化材料

摘要

Creating a desirable porous membrane with high-flux and energy-saving properties for the purification of water containing sub-micron sized contaminants, especially pathogenic microbes is of great significance, yet still a great challenge. Herein, we demonstrate a facile methodology to construct an innovative membrane with continuous cellulose Voronoi-nanonet structures via nonsolvent induced phase separation. This approach enables cellulose Voronoinanonets to tightly weld with electrospun nanofibrous substrates by controlling the solvent-nonsolvent mutual diffusion process. The resultant membranes exhibit integrated properties of small pore size (0. 23 μm), high porosity (90. 7%), good interconnectivity, and ultrathin thickness (~600 nm, two orders of magnitude thinner than the conventional microfiltration membrane). As a result, the prepared membranes can effectively intercept sub-micron particles (~0. 3 μm) with robust rejection efficiency (>99. 80%) and ultrahigh permeation flux (maximum of 8834 L m-2 h-1) under an extremely low driving pressure (≤20 kPa). More importantly, prominent bacterial rejection efficiency with log reduction value (LRV) of 8. 0 (overcoming previous limitation of LRV <7) and outstanding antifouling function are also achieved for the membranes. The successful fabrication of such a versatile membrane may provide new insights into the development of nextgeneration high-performance separation materials for various applications.

创造一个期望的有着高通量和低能耗的性能的多孔薄膜去净化水有着非常重大的意义，净化水是通过留存次微米污染物而达到的，尤其是病原体，但是该研究仍然有着巨大的挑战。在此，我们通过一个简单的方法去构建一个新型的有着连续纤维素Voronoi纳米网的结构的薄膜，该结构通过非溶剂导致的相分离而产生的。这个方法能够使得纤维素voronoi那你网去轻微的和通过溶剂-非溶剂混合分散过程而产生的静电纺丝纳米纤维基体相结合。产物薄膜展现了小孔径（0. 23 μm）、高孔隙率（90. 7%）、好的交联性和超薄的厚度（约为600 nm，比传统的微孔过滤薄膜薄两个数量级。）结果，制备的薄膜可以有效的以强大的排斥效应阻挡亚微米的粒子（约0. 3 μm）和在极低的驱动压力下（<= 20 kPa）具有极高的透过通量（最高可达8834L m-2 h-1）。更重要的是，这个薄膜首先有着8. 0的对数下降值LRV（的抗菌能力（克服了LRV小于7的限制），而且有杰出的防污的功能。这种多功能膜的成功制造可能为开发用于各种应用的下一代高性能分离材料提供新的视野。

介绍

第一段

Pathogenic microbial contamination of water for food manufacture and drug processing generates a fetal threat to human health, which has been identified as the one of the greatest global safety concerns. This issue is highlighted by a report from New Zealand’s Ministry for Primary Industries in 2013 that 38 tons whey protein was contaminated by Clostridium sporogenes, which triggered the tremendous fear about food contamination around the world. In addition, serious adverse drug events caused by microbial contamination have raised from ~30 000 to >90 000 over the past few decades, as monitored by the US Food and Drug Administration. Therefore, effective purification of the water is particularly significant and highly desired. Currently, general water purification methods to eliminate the hazard of microbial contamination include direct disinfection and heat treatment. However, the commonly used disinfectants (such as bleaching powder, hypochlorites, and chloramine) often lead to inevitable and undesirable secondary contamination, which are not recommended in water for food manufacture and drug processing. As to the heat treatment involving evaporation treatment, ultrahigh-temperature treatment and pasteurization, it can indeed remove most of the bacteria, whereas is ineffective for spore-forming bacteria and also damages the heat-sensitive components in products at the same time. Alternatively, membrane microfiltration, showing advantages over the above methods in terms of no secondary pollution and mild operating temperature (generally <50 °C), has attracted increasing attention in water purification. However, the available membranes are mainly prepared from polyvinylidene fluoride, polyether sulfone, and cellulose acetate by phase inversion, resulting in undesirable hydrophobic surface. As a result, these membranes always suffer from performance deficiencies of low permeation fluxes (<1000 L m-2 h-1) and high driving pressures (>100 kPa). Therefore, extensive efforts should be further devoted to fabricating a microfiltration membrane with high flux and low energy consumption.

食物和药物的加工过程中的水中的致病菌污染是人类健康威胁的摇篮。这已经被调整为全球安全最应该关系的问题之一。新西兰初级产业部2013年的一份报告强调了这一问题，该报告指出，38 吨乳清蛋白被产孢梭菌污染，引发了全球对食品污染的巨大担忧。而且，据美国食品药物部门报道，过去十年里，由致病菌污染导致的严重的不良药物事件已经从约30,000起上升到来大于90,000起。因此，有效的水净化材料是尤其重要的和急需的。当前，消除水中有害致病菌污染的水净化方法包括直接杀菌和热处理。然而，通常的杀菌方法（例如漂白粉、次氯酸盐、氯氨）通常会导致不可避免的不良的二次污染。这通常不推荐使用在食物和药物加工过程中的水中。高温处理包括蒸发处理、高温处理和巴氏消毒。这确实可以消除大量的细菌，但是对于孢子类型的细菌是没有效果的，同时也会损伤产品中的热刺激性化合物。另一方面，微孔过滤膜因有着在美国二次污染物和温和操作温度（通常小于50 °C）方面由于前面的几种方法而在水净化领域越来越受到关注。然而，现有的膜主要由聚偏氟乙烯、聚醚砜和醋酸纤维素通过相转化制备，导致不理想的疏水表面。 因此，这些膜总是存在低渗透通量和高驱动压力的性能缺陷。因此，应进一步致力于制造具有高通量和低能耗的微孔过滤膜。

第二段

Cellulose, with attractive merits of superhydrophilicity, easy accessibility, and renewability, has been demonstrated as an ideal candidate for fabricating high-performance microfiltration membranes. Compared with conventional hydrophobic membranes, hydrophilic cellulose- based water filtration membranes prepared from phase inversion exhibit improved fluxes (generally 1000~1500 L m-2 h-1). However, the further improvement of flux is severely hindered by the structural limitations involving low porosity (<80%), large thickness (>100 μm), and poor channel connectivity, which result from the unreasonable tailoring of phase inversion process. These structural bottlenecks have been partially overcome by loading cellulose nanofibers on electrospun nanofibrous membranes (NFMs) with high porosity and favorable interconnectivity, by impregnation or suction filtration. However, the functional cellulose layer, formed by the rearrangement of mutually independent nanofibers, is subjected to discontinuous structure. Consequently, the isolated cellulose nanofibers are easy to permeate into the porous channels of NFMs, and then resulting in a declined permeation flux, which tremendously impedes the practical applications of these cellulose nanofibers/NFMs.

有着超疏水性、获取容易和可再生的特点的纤维素已经被认为是一个优良的纤维微孔渗透膜的一个好方案。比起传统的疏水性薄膜，表现出高通量（通常为1,000到1,500L m-2 h-1）的疏水纤维基水渗透膜是通过相转化制得的。然而，未来通量的提升是被一系列的结构限制所阻止，如低的孔隙率（小于80%）、大的厚度（大于100微米）和匮乏的通道连接数。这是因为不可理的相转化过程导致的。这个结构的瓶颈已经通过在由高孔隙率和良好的交联性的静电纺丝纳米纤维膜（NFMs）载入纤维素纳米纤维通过渗透或吸出过滤来部分克服瓶颈。然而，通过同样的迪利纳米纤维重排的功能纤维素层受不连续结构的影响。而且，独立的纤维素纳米纤维是很容易的渗透过NFMs的孔隙通道。然后导致渗透通量的降低。极大的阻止了纤维素纳米纤维/NFMs的实际应用。

第三段

Herein, we present a feasible methodology for construction of highly effective and robustly water-permeable membranes with continuous Voronoi-like nanonets tightly welded on the nanofibrous substrate, for the first time, by nonsolvent induced phase separation of continuous cellulose solution. The structures of the Voronoi-nanonets are effectually designed and optimized by means of regulating the coagulation bath temperature and cellulose concentration. Attributing to such continuous cellulose Voronoi-nanonets layer, the prepared membranes demonstrate distinct structural advantages of small pore size, high porosity, ultrathin thickness, and prominent interconnectivity. Consequently, it can effectually reject sub-micron sized contaminants involving \(\ce{TiO2}\) microparticles and Escherichia coli (E. coli) with robust permeation fluxes even under ultralow pressures, also shows desirable reusability, favorable continuous filtration performance, and superior antifouling performance.

在此，我们准备了一个合适的对于高效和强有力的有着连续类Voronoi纳米网的水渗透膜的方法论。该膜是第一次通过非溶剂的引起的连续的纤维素溶液的相分离粘结在纳米纤维基体的表面。这个Voronoi纳米网的结构是高效的借助于控制温度和纤维素的集中的凝固来设计和优化。归因于一些连续的纤维素Voronoi纳米网层，制备的膜被证明有小孔径、高孔隙率、超薄层和突出了交联性的本质性能。并且，它还可以阻挡一些亚微米级别的污染物，包括\(\ce{TiO2}\)微粒和甚至在超低的压力下也有着强有力的渗透通量的Escherichia coli (E. coli)，其也展现了合格的可重复利用性、良好的连续过滤性能以及超强的抗污能力。

总结

In conclusion, we have successfully demonstrated a robust and facile strategy for fabricating membranes with continuous cellulose Voronoi-nanonets structure by nonsolvent induced phase separation. The structures of the Voronoi-nanonets were effectually controlled by facilely adjusting the coagulation bath temperature and the cellulose concentration. The continuous Voronoi-nanonets endowed the membranes with superior structures involving small pore size (0.23 μm), high porosity (90.7%), favorable interconnectivity, and ultrathin thickness (~600 nm). As a result, the prepared cellulose Voronoi-nanonet membranes exhibited a robust filtration performance for ~0.3 μm \(\ce{TiO2}\) particles with excellent rejection efficiency (>99.80%), high permeation flux (maximum of 8834 L m-2 h-1) under low driving pressures (≤20 kPa), desirable reusability and impressive continuous filtration performance. Significantly, a permeation flux of 736 L m-2 h-1 together with excellent rejection efficiency of 99.80% was achieved merely at the driving of self-gravity (≈1 kPa), which was rarely been found in other microfiltration membranes with the same pore size. Meanwhile, the obtained cellulose Voronoi-nanonet membranes also demonstrated excellent sterilization (LRV of 8.0, and permeation flux of 1444 L m-2 h-1) and antifouling performances. We hope that the successful construction of such cellulose Voronoi- nanonet membranes could provide new approach for designing the next-generation separation membranes which possess high performance and are capable of being applied in various applications such as medical health, bioengineering, and food processing.

  • self-gravity：自重力
  • sterilization：杀菌；节育

总之，我们成功的找到了一个好用简单的方法去通过非溶剂导致的相分离而制备出具有连续的纤维素Voronoi纳米网结构的纤维膜。这个Voronoi纳米网可以被有效的通过调整温度和纤维素的聚集的凝聚来控制。这个连续的Voronoi纳米网结构赋予了薄膜小孔径（0.23 μm）、高孔隙率（90.7%），良好的交联性和超薄的厚度（约600 nm）。所以，制备的纤维素Voronoi纳米网薄膜展现了优良的对于约0。3微米的\(\ce{TiO2}\)粒子的过滤效果和杰出的阻挡效率（高于99.80%）、在低驱动力（小于等于20 kPa）下的高渗透通量（最大可达8834 L m-2 h-1）、优秀的可重复利用性以及突出的连续渗透能力。显然，一个736 L m-2 h-1渗透通量和优秀的99.80%阻挡效率的东西仅仅需要自重力（约为1 kPa）。这是在其他具有相同孔径的微孔过滤膜中难以发现的。通过，易获得的纤维素Voronoi纳米网膜也被认为是有优异的杀菌和抗污能力（8.0的LRV和1444 L m-2 h-1的渗透通量）。我们希望这个成功的纤维素Voronoi纳米膜的制作可以提供一个新方法的对于涉及下一代高性能和可用于多种应用场景的分离膜，如药品安全、生物工程和食品制造过程。
